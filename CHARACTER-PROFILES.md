# Character Profiles: Terry Terry Larry Berry

## Overview
This brand acts as an underground art collective masquerading as a sticker brand. The narrative is driven by four distinct yet interconnected characters, acting almost as a fractured psyche.

---

## 1. Terry (The Sketcher)

- **Bio:** Obsessive, prolific, lines-everywhere energy. Always drawing. Never finishes. He looks at the void and sees the light. Deeply insecure but constantly searching for truth through line art.
- **Visual Identity:** His domain is hand-drawn line work, pencil or ink textures, layered and imperfect. He utilizes stark `#1A1A1A` black and `#FFE135` electric yellow. He favors the Bebas Neue font rotated slightly off-axis for high-energy outbursts.

## 2. Terry (The Philosopher)

- **Bio:** Quiet, observational. "Terry is always 2" — maybe he's the duality guy. Sees things twice. Different angle. The shadow self of the first Terry, capable of depravity but generally stoic and removed.
- **Visual Identity:** Clean, sparse, considered. Symmetrical grid balances. `#2C2C2C` charcoal gray and pure whites. Uses generous line-heights and sophisticated vocabulary, asking profound questions in `IBM Plex Mono`.

## 3. Larry (The Documentarian)

- **Bio:** Vancouver street photographer. Sharp eye. Captures moments. The middleman between Terry and Berry. The boss who always has get-rich-quick schemes, but takes the blows from both sides. A man of great ability and perception.
- **Visual Identity:** True `#0F0F0F` black and documentary `#CC0000` red. Black and white or high-contrast street photography shot at severe angles. Urgent tone, present tense, no metaphor.

## 4. Berry (The Editor)

- **Bio:** Thinks it's all too much. The skeptic. The filter. The one who says no. Homicidal maniac, absolve psychopath, but happy in suicide. In the brand narrative, she acts as the ultimate brutal curator.
- **Visual Identity:** Muted darks and `2D5F2E` forest green. Very minimal. Uses clean, functional layout elements that support the text but never compete with the content.
