# Terry Community Orchestration Skill

## Core Strategy

Community is embedded in the work itself. The stickers and files invite participation. People add to them, remix them, make them part of their own practice. Yours demand engagement. That means organic growth — people showing their friends because they're part of it now.

You're in East Van, which is culturally hot right now. Street art, underground collectives, artist-run spaces — that's currency. You're not fighting against the zeitgeist. You're native to it.

## Community Building Framework

1. **Free + Paid Funnel**
   Mix free and paid relentlessly. Free sticker downloads, free sketches, free stories. Then occasional premium packs. Creates a funnel where people who love the free stuff naturally buy the paid stuff.
2. **Hashtag Campaigns**
   Establish challenges using `#eastvan`, `#terryterry`, `#addmore`. Get followers actually making art with the stickers and posting it back.
3. **Beyond Stickers**
   Expand monetization to prints, zines, commissioned street art, workshops, and potentially a Discord where the three characters hang out and chat with followers.

## Content Coordination

- The Sketcher releases unfinished lines (download the SVG).
- The Photographer provides context (the blank space).
- The Philosopher asks the prompt.
- The Editor curates the responses from the community to highlight.

**Metrics to track:** Engagement via remixes, challenge participations, and photo tags.
